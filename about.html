ABOUT US

Try to think of a financial services company that you feel truly cares about your success. Now imagine what values and ethics that firm would encompass. ETF Capital Management was founded in 2006 by Larry Berman and his partners for just that purpose: Our passion, and mission, is to empower Canadian investors with education, and help them achieve their lifestyle and retirement goals with portfolio strategies that are powerful, yet easily understood.

ETFCM is one of Canada’s leading independent wealth management firms with expertise

Using an integrated, client-centric approach, we develop long-term client relationships based on individual attention, professionalism and integrity. Our portfolios are customized to your personal needs, designed to complement your Financial Life Strategy, as well as adapt to changing global and economic opportunities while reducing risk. We are portfolio managers and fiduciaries: Our clients’ interests always come first.

Our goal is investment excellence with client peace-of-mind.
We achieve this in 3 ways:

Focus

Focus on capital preservation

Tactical

Tactical investment strategies during all market cycles

Reliable

Reliable portfolio performance

Our Business Philosophy

ETF Capital Management believes in bringing fairness back to the investment industry. We put our clients’ interests ahead of our own and treat all clients equally and offer our private clients the same advantages generally reserved for ultra-high-net-worth and institutional investors. Investor education is paramount. We want each of our clients to understand our approach and the performance achieved.

They are difficult to understand and are appropriate only for experienced investors. You should ensure that you have both the relevant experience and knowledge of the underlying asset, market and types of speculative instruments.
